Cooling Coil Valve - CCV01

Mechanical Cooling Mode

On a rise in discharge air temperature above target and if the conditions for economizer cooling are not satisfied, the cooling coil valve will modulate to maintain the discharge air temperature at its target.

Safeties

Through software the chilled water valve will go to its full open position if the freezestat trips.

Cooling Coil Valve - CCV02

Mechanical Cooling Mode

On a rise in discharge air temperature above target and if the conditions for economizer cooling are not satisfied, the cooling coil valve will modulate to maintain the discharge air temperature at its target. If the unit is in the dehumidification mode the cooling coil valve will be indexed to its full open position. The cooling coil valve will return to its normal control sequence when the unit goes out of the dehumidification mode.

Safeties

Through software the chilled water valve will go to its full open position if the freezestat trips.
